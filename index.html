Welcome to Vara Thai

Our specialty is Traditional Thai Massage, also known as "Thai Yoga Massage". Our signature massage incorporates elements of acupressure, muscle compression, assisted yoga-like stretching and deep tissue bodywork to create a unique, revitalizing whole body experience. We offer other types of massage treatments like Thai Aromatherapy, Thai Combination and Sports Massage.
